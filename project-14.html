WAVES PREMIER LEAGUE

   Looking for some strategical and fun game amidst the technical waves? Do you believe that you watch enough cricket to create the best premier league squad there is? Are you sick of watching auctions in television and want to participate in one yourself? If the answer to any of these questions is yes, then this event is definitely for you as it gives you the ultimate experience of auctioning, resource management, tactics and lots of cricket!

Introduction

   It is a premier league auction based event consisting of two rounds, with the preliminary round being pen and paper round consisting of questions related to cricket. Based on the scores in the preliminary round, teams are called upon for the second round. The second round is a real life simulation of the IPL auction. The winner and runner up are decided based on how efficiently they use the resources given to them.

Rules and Regulations

1. A team can have a maximum of 2 people.
2. Organizers decision is final
3. Individual participation and cross college teams are also allowed.
