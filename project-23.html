Introduction

   Are you an electrical nerd who is passionately curious to visualize how circuits work? Bored with theoretical lessons? Let's break the potential barrier and make it more interesting through MATLAB modeling. We dare you to insulate yourself out of the electrical stress we give to your brain on the command window.

Format


Round-1 :  Pen and Paper
Participants will be given MCQs from which they have to choose the correct answer.
Round-2 :  Clue follower
This round will contain 3 sub rounds in which the solution to each circuit in the previous sub round will act as a clue to the next sub round.
Round-3 : Tame the circuit
Finally, the participants will be asked to design circuits using MATLAB.

Rules and Regulations

1. Each team should contain a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 3 participants
2. Everyone should have your respective Waves ID.
3. Cross-college groups are allowed.
