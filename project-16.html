WAVES MEGA QUIZ

“Quizzing is a sport
       For a trained brain”
Bring your grey cell to party with “waves mega quiz”. Away from classroom rote, this quiz is all about the world of science and technology. Get ready for a roller coaster ride into the intense world of techno quiz as exciting prizes are awaiting you!

Format

Prelims -  It is a pen and paper round. Technical questions from the electrical and electronics field will be asked.
Finals -  It comprises of two rounds.
Round-1 :  It is a rapid fire round.
Round-2 :  It will be like a buzzer round.

Rules and Regulations

1. A team of maximum 3 can participate.
2. Individual participation and cross college teams are also allowed.
3. Team members are not allowed to change or swap among other teams after the prelims round.
