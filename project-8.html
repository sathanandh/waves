GOOGLE GURU

    Are you someone who spends most of your time searching in google for answers and gaining knowledge? Here's the perfect event for you to see how effectively you use google which is the most popular search engine.Participate in google guru and enjoy the whole event.

Description

Google guru has two rounds.
Round-1: The first round is pen and paper round to test the basic knowledge of participants. The shortlisted contestants from round 1 will move to round 2.
Round-2 : This round is to stimulate your adrenaline gland to rush up to google and find the keywords relating to your answers.
