MACHINE-O-MANIA

Machines and Humans at war!!! Join us in this battle of brains and show us your caliber by outwitting the machines in the smartest way possible.You may never know, this may turn out to be YOUR EVENT too.
Format :

The questions for all the rounds will be based on basic of machines and power electronics.

  • Round 1 :
    Participants will be attending a written test in which questions related to spellings in the form of crossword puzzles, jumbled words, opposites etc. will be asked.

  • Round 2 : The top 4 contestants from 1 st round will get to play in a fun-filled board game with many twists and turns. Guess what? It’ll all be related to spellings.

  • Round 3 : The shortlisted teams will be asked to design the solution of a given real life problem and integrate the same using an appropriate converter.

Rules :

  • A team can have maximum of 3 members.

  • The team must have valid waves Id to participate.

  • Both electrical and non-electrical students are welcome to participate.

  • Any malpractice would result in disqualification of the team.

  • Organizers decision will be final.
